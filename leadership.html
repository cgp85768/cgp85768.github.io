Leadership

Spirit Prefect
Spirit Prefect was a student leadership opportunity given through a competitive application process including an essay and an interview. I picked themes for all sporting events, planned activities to help students interact during sporting events, planned and ran pep rallies, led the student section, made signs and posters, and created events for different sports for the whole school to enjoy. I also ran meetings twice a month with a committee.
Student Ambassador
As a student ambassador, I was responsible for welcoming, interacting, and touring new families around the school. I also participated in a "Life of a Student" video for Open House.

Youth Leadership Summit
I was selected from teachers and staff to participate in a leadership conference with students from all around Whitfield County Schools to learn how to become a better leader
Toastmasters
Toastmasters International is a non-profit educational organization that teaches public speaking and leadership skills through a worldwide network of clubs. This was a program where I practiced public speaking, improved my communication, and built leadership skills.
Leader of Pep club
I was the president of Pep Club which was in charge of making homecoming signs, painting faces at games, making signs for games and pep rallies, making locker decorations, and creating the high school monthly calendar.
Student Section
I helped lead the student section for 3 years, where I picked out themes and led the student section with cheers for football and basketball games.
Leadership Retreat
This was a school retreat with a few other students to grow as leaders and learn how to improve our leadership skills.
